About Me

Hello! My name is Richard Tremblay, my friends and family call me RJ. I am currently studying Computer Science at the University Of Windsor. I intend to focus my studies on AI and graduate in four years with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in artificial intelligence. Throughout these next four years I plan to tailor my studies toward the acquisition and implementation of the knowledge and skills needed in order to be a powerful asset to any employer within my field.

So far my time as a student has provided me with a comfortable grasp on the fundamentals of computer science. The classes I have completed have provided me technical experience in algorithms, calculus and linear algebra, discrete mathematics, and programming. These courses have provided a strong guidance towards honing my discipline, adaptability, and problem solving skills, as well as given me direct experience in understanding the nature of computers and how we as computer scientists use them to accomplish things outside of human ability.

Outside of my profession I enjoy a variety of hobbies and activities. Im naturally a very curious person so my interests tend to vary widely. Some of my favourite things to do include: cooking, reading, travelling, playing videogames, training boxing and Muay Thai as well as jiu jitsu at a more casual level, weight training, and many other smaller things. I like to keep busy and try to make use of my time the best I can, whether it be doing the dishes or going to the gym, I like to stay busy. I do this by starting each day organized with a list of goals to complete by bedtime. This organization and tendency to keep busy in my day to day life has blended greatly into my adaptation to university life. The fast pace and dense workload keeps me busy and filled with drive and purpose.
